Key Definitions

“ATLAS” is the in-game currency token and is used for gaming purposes, such as purchasing resources or participating in the in-game decentralized financial market.

“POLIS” is a multifunctional governance token, with internal and external uses. Internally, the POLIS token gives an owner jurisdictional ownership over entire regions of in-game area. Externally, the token gives the owner the ability to influence decision making by the game development team.

“Ownership” means players are able to transact with their ATLAS and POLIS tokens using their private keys.

“Smart Contracts” means the computer code executed in response to transactions published on the Solana blockchain that generates a consistent and verifiable result.

“Automata S.A.” means the Panamanian Corporation that owns the Star Atlas App and Site.

The App

Real-time graphics technology using Unreal Engine 5’s Nanite allows for cinematic quality video game visuals. Blockchain technology using the Solana protocol proves a largely serverless and secured gameplay experience. Non-fungible tokens obtained and traded within Star Atlas create an economy that replicates the tangibility of real world assets and ownership.
